TCE Thiagarajar College of Engineering is a government-aided autonomous institution located in Madurai, Tamil Nadu, India.
The courses offered in TCE are approved by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi.

ACADEMIC PROGRAMS TCE offers 8 undergraduate,16 postgraduate programs and research programs (MS and PhD) in all Engineering and Science streams.

RANKING TCE was ranked 56 among engineering colleges in India by the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) in 2019.
